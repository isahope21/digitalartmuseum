let's collab

sooo, here's the dream:

a digital zine of works by a ton of different people. i will collect everything for us to have a space to express ourselves and you could add your name, stay anonymous, connect your art accounts. i'm in the stage of development and would love your suggestions. (i'm in ux guys i swear the user is everything to me).

show me your poetry! show me your photography! your pottery! the little plastic dinos you painted! the page of the book you annotated! whatever you find beautiful, whatever makes you feel, whatever you love, share it with me and i'll add it to my blog. still getting used to that phrase haha. "my blog". i feel like the dog from that one show that types away at night with his fake paws.

i have a strong belief that creating is important. i want to support you. and before you start doubting yourself, thinking about dimensions and sizes, or wondering if anyone will like it, know that i will cherish it like it's an acorn i picked up off the fall ground. i promise i'm not a scary gal.

so yeah...

if you're interested in this coffee-charged dream of mine, send me an email or send my art account a dm. i would love to hear from you!
